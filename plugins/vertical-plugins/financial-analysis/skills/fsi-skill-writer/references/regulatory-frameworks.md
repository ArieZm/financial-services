# Regulatory Frameworks Reference

## By Jurisdiction

### United States

#### SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission)
- **Reg FD** — Fair Disclosure: material nonpublic information must be disclosed broadly
- **Reg AC** — Analyst Certification: research analysts must certify views are their own
- **Reg BI** — Best Interest: broker-dealers must act in retail customer's best interest
- **Rule 17a-4** — Record retention: electronic communications and records preservation
- **Form CRS** — Customer Relationship Summary: plain-language disclosure to retail investors

#### FINRA
- **Rule 2241** — Equity research independence and conflicts
- **Rule 2242** — Debt research independence
- **Rule 2111** — Suitability: reasonable-basis, customer-specific, quantitative
- **Rule 3110** — Supervision and compliance systems
- **Communications rules (2210)** — Fair, balanced, not misleading

#### Banking / AML
- **BSA (Bank Secrecy Act)** — Anti-money laundering program requirements
- **CDD Rule (31 CFR 1010.230)** — Customer Due Diligence, beneficial ownership
- **CIP** — Customer Identification Program (Section 326 of USA PATRIOT Act)
- **OFAC** — Sanctions screening (SDN list, sectoral sanctions)
- **SAR filing** — Suspicious Activity Reports for transactions > thresholds

#### SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley)
- **Section 302** — CEO/CFO certification of financial statements
- **Section 404** — Internal controls over financial reporting (ICFR)
- **Section 802** — Document retention and destruction

### European Union

#### MiFID II / MiFIR
- **Best execution** — Firms must take sufficient steps for best client outcomes
- **Research unbundling** — Separate payment for research from execution
- **Transaction reporting** — T+1 reporting to national competent authorities
- **Product governance** — Target market assessment for each product

#### GDPR
- **Data minimization** — Only process data necessary for the stated purpose
- **Right to erasure** — Delete personal data on request
- **Consent** — Explicit consent for processing personal data
- **Cross-border transfer** — Adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses

### International

#### Basel III/IV
- **Capital requirements** — CET1, Tier 1, Total Capital ratios
- **Leverage ratio** — Non-risk-weighted capital measure
- **Liquidity (LCR/NSFR)** — Short-term and structural liquidity requirements
- **Credit risk** — Standardised and IRB approaches

#### ILPA (Institutional Limited Partners Association)
- **Fee reporting template** — Standardized LP fee/expense disclosure
- **Performance reporting** — IRR, TVPI, DPI, RVPI conventions
- **GP transparency** — Portfolio company monitoring, ESG reporting

## By Workflow Type

### Investment Banking Pitches
- Insider trading walls (material nonpublic information handling)
- Fair and balanced communications (FINRA 2210)
- Proper disclaimers on forward-looking statements
- No guarantees of investment outcomes

### Equity Research
- Reg AC certification language required
- FINRA 2241 conflict disclosures (ownership, banking relationship)
- Rating distribution disclosure
- Price target methodology disclosure

### Wealth Management
- Reg BI / suitability documentation
- Form CRS delivery requirements
- Fee transparency
- Risk disclosure appropriate to client sophistication

### Fund Administration
- AICPA SOC 1/SOC 2 reporting standards
- GIPS (Global Investment Performance Standards) for performance
- ILPA fee reporting guidelines
- LP statement accuracy and timeliness

### KYC / Operations
- BSA/AML program elements (CIP, CDD, EDD, ongoing monitoring)
- OFAC screening requirements
- SAR filing thresholds and timing
- Record retention (5 years minimum under BSA)

## Skill Writing Implications

When a skill touches regulated workflows:

1. **Include compliance guardrails** — Explicit instructions to flag regulatory concerns
2. **Add disclosure templates** — Standard regulatory language for outputs
3. **Note data handling requirements** — What can/cannot be stored, shared, or cached
4. **Reference record retention** — If outputs must be retained, note the requirement
5. **Flag review checkpoints** — Where human compliance review is mandatory before output distribution
