# Financial Test Scenarios

Template test scenarios organized by vertical and difficulty. Use these as starting points for eval test case generation.

## Investment Banking

### Easy
- "Build trading comps for Apple (AAPL) against large-cap tech peers"
- "Create a sector overview for US regional banking"
- "Draft a teaser for a hypothetical sale of a $500M revenue SaaS company"

### Medium
- "Build a pitch deck for Microsoft acquiring a cybersecurity company. Include comps, precedents, and a DCF"
- "Model a merger of two mid-cap industrial companies. Show accretion/dilution at various exchange ratios"
- "Create a CIM for a private healthcare services company with $200M revenue and 15% EBITDA margins"

### Hard
- "Build a full pitch for a cross-border acquisition: European acquirer buying a US target. Address FX, regulatory, and tax considerations"
- "Model a carve-out of a division from a conglomerate. Value the parent, the SpinCo, and show RemainCo impact"
- "Advise on a hostile takeover defense: analyze the target's vulnerabilities, potential white knights, and defense mechanisms"

## Equity Research

### Easy
- "Write up AAPL's Q3 earnings. Revenue beat by 3%, EPS in line. Services grew 15% YoY"
- "Update the MSFT model for the latest quarter. Adjust Azure growth from 25% to 28%"

### Medium
- "Draft an initiation of coverage on CrowdStrike. Bull case: platform consolidation. Bear case: competition from Microsoft"
- "Analyze the impact of new tariffs on US auto manufacturers. Which OEMs are most exposed?"

### Hard
- "Build a sum-of-the-parts valuation for Alphabet. Separate: Search/Ads, Cloud, YouTube, Waymo, Other Bets"
- "Write a sector note on US banks ahead of Fed rate decision. Model NIM sensitivity for the top 6 banks"

## Private Equity

### Easy
- "Screen for acquisition targets in US business services with $50-200M revenue and >20% EBITDA margins"
- "Build a quick LBO model for a target at 8x EBITDA, 5x leverage, $100M EBITDA"

### Medium
- "Prepare an IC memo for a platform acquisition in veterinary services. Include market sizing, roll-up thesis, and 5-year returns"
- "Model a dividend recapitalization for a portfolio company after 2 years. The company has de-levered from 5x to 3x"

### Hard
- "Build a full add-on acquisition model: existing platform + bolt-on target. Show combined returns with and without synergies"
- "Analyze a secondary sale of LP interests in a PE fund. Model the discount to NAV and implied returns for the buyer"

## Wealth Management

### Easy
- "Prepare a quarterly portfolio review for a client with 60/40 allocation. Compare performance to benchmarks"
- "Run a basic financial plan: client is 45, earns $300K, wants to retire at 65 with $10M"

### Medium
- "Analyze whether a client should do a Roth conversion this year. Current income $500K, expected retirement income $200K"
- "Rebalance a $5M portfolio that has drifted from target: US equities are 10% overweight, fixed income is underweight"

### Hard
- "Design a multi-generational wealth transfer plan. $50M estate, 3 children, 7 grandchildren. Consider GRATs, IDGTs, and family LPs"
- "Build a concentrated stock diversification plan for a tech executive with $20M in company stock and 10b5-1 plan constraints"

## Fund Administration

### Easy
- "Reconcile a simple GL with 20 line items. Opening balance $1M, 15 matched items, 5 breaks"
- "Calculate NAV for a fund with 10 positions. Use provided fair values"

### Medium
- "Prepare a quarterly LP statement for a PE fund. Show capital account, performance metrics (IRR, TVPI, DPI), and fee summary"
- "Run month-end close: calculate accruals for management fee, admin expenses, and audit fee. Post journal entries"

### Hard
- "Perform a full waterfall distribution calculation for a PE fund with European-style waterfall, 8% preferred return, 20% carry, and catch-up"
- "Audit LP statements across 3 related funds with cross-fund investing. Verify allocation methodology and fee netting"

## Operations / KYC

### Easy
- "Parse a standard KYC packet for a US corporation. Extract entity name, EIN, beneficial owners, and formation state"
- "Screen a company name against the OFAC SDN list"

### Medium
- "Process a KYC packet for a foreign entity with complex ownership. Identify UBOs through 3 layers of holding companies"
- "Evaluate a high-risk client onboarding: PEP exposure, negative news screening, source of wealth documentation"

### Hard
- "Design a rules-based AML monitoring framework for a fund admin. Define thresholds for wire transfers, cash transactions, and velocity"
- "Perform enhanced due diligence on a trust structure with beneficiaries in 4 jurisdictions. Map regulatory requirements per jurisdiction"
